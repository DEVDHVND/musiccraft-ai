            Pro Tips
          
    
          
    
            
    
              No artist names
              Suno blocks direct references — use genre + era descriptors instead (already handled).
            
    
            
    
              Front-load genre
              Suno weighs earlier words more — genre always goes first in the style prompt.
            
    
            
    
              4–8 descriptors
              Too few = generic output. Too many = conflicting signals. This tool targets the sweet spot.
            
    
            
    
              Style field limit
              Suno v4.5+ supports 1000 chars. Stay green (under 800) for best results. Amber = close to limit.
